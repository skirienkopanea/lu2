Rules:

  • Player 1 (red) always starts.
  • Players must roll the dice by clicking on 'ROLL DICE' to evaluate the cell moves.
  • Clicking on your tokens will highlight the possible cell move for that token based on the dice number
  • You may click on another token to display its assigned possible cell move
  • The last token selected is displayed darker. Clicking on the highlighted ('yellow background') cell will execute the move
  • Any dice number is valid to get a token outside the base, and move it the number of cells determined by the dice. Starting at the black arrowed with same background color as the player's base cell.
  • The goal of the game is to bring all tokens to the star theme cell (aka home cell) of the player's color. The first player to do so wins the game.
  • Pieces move around the external cells of the board clockwise and must make a turn to the central home column of the player's color. The turn is determined by the white cell with an arrow of your color. That cell is also part of the players path.
  • When a token lands on an occupied cell the old token is sent back to it's base, regardless of the team of the coin (so you will send your own tokens back as well as the opponents').
  • Tokens don't block the path in this Ludo variant. Tokens may jump over a token placed before the landing cell
  • The home (star) cell has special rules. Tokens landing on the home cell won't send back the current tokens occupying the home cell. If you want to move a token to the home cell make sure to not click on another coin in the home. Therefore instead of clicking in the center of the home cell just click around the edges of the home cell (anywhere where there's room left to click the home cell without clicking another coin)
  • If the dice number is bigger or equal than the number of cells required to reach the home cell, then the possible move for such token is the home cell.
  • A player may make a move within 30 seconds, if no move is made in 30 seconds the turn is given to the opposite player.
  • The game may also finish if no winer is determiend after 1 hour. That will make it a draw.
  • The game may also finished by abortion if a player abandons the game site or if it clicks on abort game. There is no winner in that case, just bad sportmanship.
